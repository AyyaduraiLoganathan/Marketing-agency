E-Commerce SEO Campaign

Project Overview

Our client, an e-commerce retailer specializing in home goods, was struggling with low organic traffic and poor search engine rankings. We developed a comprehensive SEO strategy to improve their online visibility and drive more qualified traffic to their website.

The Challenge

The client faced several challenges, including:

  • Poor website structure and technical SEO issues
  • Limited keyword targeting and content strategy
  • Strong competition in a saturated market
  • Low domain authority and backlink profile

Our Approach

We implemented a multi-faceted SEO strategy that included:

  • Comprehensive technical SEO audit and implementation of fixes
  • In-depth keyword research and content optimization
  • Creation of new category and product pages with optimized content
  • Strategic link building campaign to improve domain authority
  • Local SEO optimization to target regional customers

Results

200%

Increase in organic traffic

85%

Improvement in keyword rankings

150%

Increase in organic conversions

Within six months of implementing our SEO strategy, the client saw significant improvements in their organic search performance, resulting in increased traffic, higher rankings, and more conversions.
